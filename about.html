About Az Visa Consultancy

Your trusted partner in global opportunities for over a decade

Our Story

Founded in 2013, Az Visa Consultancy began with a simple mission: to make international opportunities accessible to everyone. What started as a small office in Dhaka has grown into one of Bangladesh's most trusted visa consultancy firms.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of individuals and families achieve their dreams of working, studying, and settling abroad. Our success is built on transparency, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success.

Today, we continue to expand our services and partnerships, always staying at the forefront of immigration policy changes and global employment trends.
