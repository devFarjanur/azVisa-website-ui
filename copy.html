Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our visa and immigration services

What documents do I need for a work visa application?

The specific documents required vary by country and visa type, but generally include:

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Completed visa application form
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Employment contract or job offer letter
  • Educational certificates and transcripts
  • Professional experience certificates
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Medical examination reports
  • Proof of financial means

Our consultants will provide you with a customized document checklist based on your specific situation and destination country.

How long does the visa processing take?

Processing times vary depending on the country and type of visa:

  • UAE Work Visa: 2-3 weeks
  • Poland Work Visa: 4-6 weeks
  • Canada Work Permit: 8-12 weeks
  • UK Work Visa: 3-6 weeks
  • Australia Skilled Visa: 6-9 months
  • Singapore Employment Pass: 3-8 weeks

These are average processing times and can vary based on individual circumstances and embassy workload. We provide regular updates on your application status throughout the process.

What is your success rate for visa approvals?

We maintain an overall success rate of 98% across all our visa services. Here's our success rate by visa type:

  • UAE Work Visa: 95% success rate
  • Poland Work Visa: 92% success rate
  • Canada Study Visa: 90% success rate
  • UK Work Visa: 88% success rate
  • Australia Skilled Migration: 85% success rate
  • Documentation Services: 98% success rate

Our high success rate is attributed to our thorough document verification, expert guidance, and personalized approach to each application.

Do you provide job placement services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive job placement services for selected countries and industries. Our job placement services include:

  • Job matching based on your skills and experience
  • Interview preparation and coaching
  • Resume/CV writing services
  • Employer introduction and referral
  • Salary negotiation support
  • Pre-departure orientation

We have partnerships with employers in various sectors across our destination countries. However, job placement is subject to your qualifications, experience, and market availability.

What happens if my visa application is rejected?

In the rare event that your visa application is rejected, we provide the following support:

  • Detailed analysis of the rejection reasons
  • Guidance on addressing the issues that led to rejection
  • Assistance with preparing a stronger application for re-submission
  • Appeal process guidance where applicable
  • Alternative visa options exploration

We offer a partial refund or credit toward future services according to our refund policy. Our team will work closely with you to understand the reasons for rejection and develop a strategy for a successful reapplication.

How much do your services cost?

Our service fees vary based on the type of visa, destination country, and complexity of your case. Here's an overview of our pricing structure:

  • Free Initial Consultation: No charge
  • UAE Work Visa: Starting from $500
  • Poland Work Visa: Starting from $800
  • Canada Work Permit: Starting from $1,200
  • UK Work Visa: Starting from $1,000
  • Documentation Services: Starting from $300

These fees are exclusive of government application fees, which vary by country. We provide a detailed breakdown of all costs during your consultation. We also offer payment plans for eligible clients.
