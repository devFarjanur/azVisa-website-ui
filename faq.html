UAE Visa Questions

What is the processing time for a UAE work visa?

The standard processing time for a UAE work visa is approximately 2-3 weeks from the date of application submission. However, this can vary depending on the emirate, type of employment, and current embassy workload. We provide regular updates throughout the process.

What are the medical requirements for UAE employment visa?

All UAE employment visa applicants must undergo a medical examination which includes:

  • Chest X-ray for tuberculosis screening
  • Blood test for HIV/AIDS, hepatitis B and C, and syphilis
  • General health check-up
  • Additional tests may be required for specific professions

We coordinate the medical examination at approved centers and handle all related documentation.

Can I change jobs in the UAE after getting a work visa?

Yes, under the current UAE labor laws, employees can change jobs after completing their contract period (typically 2 years) or by mutual agreement with the employer. There are specific procedures to follow for job transfer, including obtaining a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from your current employer and applying for a new work permit. We can assist with job transfer procedures when needed.

Poland Visa Questions

What types of work visas are available for Poland?

Poland offers several types of work visas and permits:

  • Type A Work Permit: For employment with a Polish employer
  • Type B Work Permit: For board members in Polish companies
  • Type C Work Permit: For foreign employers sending employees to Poland
  • Type D Work Permit: For seasonal employment (up to 9 months)
  • Type E Work Permit: For intra-company transfers
  • EU Blue Card: For highly qualified professionals

We help determine the most suitable option based on your qualifications and employment situation.

How long does it take to get a Poland work visa?

The processing time for a Poland work visa typically takes 60-90 days from application submission. This includes:

  • Work permit application processing: 30-60 days
  • Visa application after work permit approval: 15-30 days

We monitor the application status closely and provide regular updates throughout the process.

Can my family join me in Poland?

Yes, family reunification is possible in Poland. After obtaining your work permit and visa, your immediate family members (spouse and minor children) can apply for residence permits to join you. The requirements include:

  • Proof of relationship (marriage certificate, birth certificates)
  • Proof of adequate accommodation in Poland
  • Proof of sufficient financial means to support family members
  • Valid health insurance

We provide comprehensive assistance for family reunification applications.

Document Questions

What documents are typically required for visa applications?

While requirements vary by country and visa type, common documents include:

  • Valid passport (with at least 6 months validity)
  • Passport-sized photographs meeting specific requirements
  • Completed visa application forms
  • Educational certificates and transcripts
  • Professional experience certificates
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Medical examination reports
  • Proof of financial means
  • Employment contract or job offer letter
  • Bank statements

We provide a customized document checklist based on your specific visa category and destination country.

Do I need to get my documents translated?

Yes, most countries require that documents not in their official language be translated by a certified translator. The requirements vary by country:

  • Poland: Documents must be translated into Polish by a sworn translator
  • UAE: Documents in English are generally accepted, Arabic translation may be required for certain documents
  • Canada: English or French translations required
  • UK: English translations required

We work with certified translation services to ensure your documents meet the specific requirements of each country.
